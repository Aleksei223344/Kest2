Why would it be beneficial to add snap-ins to the MMC that are for other computers on the network?

* It allows remote management of other computers without physically accessing them.
* You can monitor, configure, and troubleshoot multiple systems from a single console, saving time and improving efficiency.
* Helps with centralized administration in enterprise environments.



After restarting the computer, why was the desktop color changed?

* The change was saved in the Registry under HKEY\_CURRENT\_USER → Control Panel → Colors → Background.
* Windows reads this value at logon/startup, so the desktop color reflects the value stored in the registry.
